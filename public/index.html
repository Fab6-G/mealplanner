💡 Inventory Tips

  • Update Weekly: Check on Sundays to plan next week's shopping
  • FIFO Method: Use older items first (First In, First Out)
  • Smart Storage: Keep proteins frozen, vegetables in crisper
  • Prep Ahead: Batch cook on Sundays using your inventory
  • Waste Prevention: Dashboard will suggest recipes based on expiring items
